About the Blanket Drive

The Warm Hearts Blanket Drive is a compassionate initiative dedicated to collecting and distributing blankets to the most vulnerable members of our community during the harsh winter months. Our mission is simple: to ensure that no one suffers from the cold.

  • Your blanket can save someone from freezing temperatures.
  • We work with local volunteers to distribute donations efficiently.
  • Transparent use of every contribution, ensuring maximum impact.
  • A community-driven effort to bring comfort and dignity to all.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will my blankets reach people?

We have a robust network of local volunteers who identify areas and individuals most in need, including homeless shelters, roadside dwellers, and impoverished communities. Blankets are distributed directly to ensure they reach the intended beneficiaries efficiently.

Can I donate from outside India?

Currently, our primary donation method is UPI, which is primarily for Indian bank accounts. However, if you are an international donor interested in contributing, please reach out to us via the contact form, and we can discuss alternative methods.

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes, Warm Hearts Blanket Drive is a registered NGO. Donations made to us are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a donation receipt via email after your contribution.

How can I volunteer?

We always welcome enthusiastic volunteers! Please fill out the contact form on our website with your interest in volunteering, and our team will get in touch with you to discuss opportunities and next steps.

How often do you conduct drives?

Our primary blanket distribution drives run throughout the winter months, typically from November to February. However, we also conduct smaller, targeted outreach programs year-round based on specific needs and donations received.
